My Journey with Knowledge & Quizzing

My love affair with knowledge began with an insatiable appetite for reading. Devouring information never felt like a chore, and my dedication led me to excel in school quiz competitions. This early success fueled my desire to delve deeper, leading me to appreciate the systematic organization of knowledge.

A Journey of Accolades: My quizzing journey has been punctuated by numerous awards. I held the coveted title of unbeaten college quiz champion. Beyond college, I twice emerged victorious in the prestigious Sargalayam State Competition, claiming the title of All Kerala Quiz Champion.

From Quizzer to Quiz Master: Recognizing a gap in specialized quiz hosting, I stepped up to become the Quiz Master for my college. This initial success propelled me forward, leading to opportunities to conduct the Kozhikode District Quiz Competition and invitations to other institutions.
